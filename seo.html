Search Engine Optimization

What is Search Engine Optimization?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is an internet marketing strategy that enables websites that rank for relevant and similar keywords or key phrases organically on the search engine results pages (SERPs). The main advantage of SEO is that businesses will get more traffic to their websites, which results in more enquiries

Method

SEO is a process that improves your website’s position on search engine results pages (SERPs).

When search engines crawl and index your page, SEO helps them to understand your site. Hence, your website will be deemed as most relevant for a given user search enquiry.

Searches

To attain a good website quality score, SEO requires the combine efforts of HTML coding, website design, copywriting and quality content.

By understanding your target audience’s needs and their search tendencies, your website can be customized for their needs.

Lead Generation

SEO is the foundation of all internet marketing efforts. 90% of users click on results on the first page, as such a high ranking is crucial for generating conversions.

Additionally, SEO efforts do help to boost SEM campaigns as your site’s content is judged to be of higher quality.
